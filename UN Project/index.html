Methodology & data standards

This atlas is built to the standards of an institutional research resource. Every number displayed is tied to a primary source that is either (a) produced by an intergovernmental body or government statistical agency, or (b) published in a recognised comparative research programme.

Tiered sourcing

  • Tier 1 (institutional): ITU, UNESCO, World Bank, UN Data Portal, WHO, UNDP.
  • Tier 2 (comparative research): Reuters Institute Digital News Report, Pew Research, DataReportal, GSMA, regional barometers (Afro, Latino, Asian, Euro, Arab), RSF, Freedom House.
  • Tier 3 (aggregators, cross-checked): Our World in Data; traced back to primary in every case.

Refresh cadence

Automated indicators refresh daily via public APIs (World Bank, ITU). Manual-update indicators refresh once a year when flagship reports (Reuters Institute DNR, RSF, Freedom House) publish new editions.

Confidence flags

Each cell carries a confidence flag (high / medium / low / estimated / preliminary). Any cell marked preliminary has not yet been independently verified against its primary source and must not be cited publicly.
